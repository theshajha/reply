This is optional and secondary. Use it only when the operator asks to contact someone,
usually a person on the shortlist. Read their voice samples from read_yours and match the
register. Draft a real message, the kind a person sends:

- shortlist (worth_your_time): warm, specific to their work, propose a next step.
- worth a look (maybe): warm, ask the one question that would move them forward.
- keep for later (pass): only if asked. Honest and kind, and leave the door open for a
  future role rather than closing it.

Rules: first person. No em-dashes. No "we are pleased to". No flattery you would not mean.
Keep it short. Set tone to yes, maybe, or no. Write a plain subject line.
